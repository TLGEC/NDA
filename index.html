1. Purpose

The receiving party may be shown, told about, or given access to confidential information relating to a survey app, sales workflow, customer journey, product concept, templates, pricing approach, operational process, demonstration material, documentation, designs, code structure, data capture approach, export packs, commercial plans and related know-how owned or controlled by James Cooling.

2. Confidential information

Confidential information includes anything disclosed verbally, visually, electronically, in writing, by demonstration, by screen share, by test access, by document, by image, by video, or by discussion that relates to the app, product, process, business model, commercial opportunity or supporting materials.

3. Obligations

The receiving party agrees to:

  • keep the confidential information private and secure;
  • use it only for the purpose of evaluating or discussing the opportunity with James Cooling;
  • not copy, record, screenshot, screen-record, reproduce, reverse engineer, share, publish or disclose it without written permission;
  • not use the information to build, commission, assist, market or improve a competing product or process;
  • only share the information with advisers or colleagues where strictly necessary and only where they are bound by equivalent confidentiality duties.

4. Exclusions

This agreement does not apply to information that the receiving party can prove was already lawfully known to them, is public through no breach of this agreement, is independently developed without using the confidential information, or is received lawfully from a third party without confidentiality restrictions.

5. Ownership

All intellectual property, concepts, designs, workflows, documents, demonstrations, code, app logic, templates, commercial material and related rights remain the property of James Cooling or his licensors. No licence, assignment or right to use the information is granted except for the limited evaluation purpose above.

6. Return and deletion

On request, the receiving party must promptly return or delete confidential information, including copies, notes, extracts, screenshots, recordings, downloads or derivative material, except where retention is required by law or for professional compliance records.

7. Term

The confidentiality obligations continue for five years from the date of acceptance. Trade secrets and highly sensitive technical or commercial information remain protected for as long as they remain confidential.

8. No obligation to proceed

Nothing in this agreement requires either party to proceed with any transaction, partnership, licence, sale, pilot or commercial arrangement.

9. Governing law

This agreement is governed by the laws of England and Wales, and the courts of England and Wales shall have jurisdiction.
